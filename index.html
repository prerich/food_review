Jen's Favorites

Here's some of my favorite places to eat! :)

Tsaocaa Malden

Category Score
Food 10
Interior 9
Prices/Affordability 6

Tsaocaa is a boba shop. I have only been to the Tsaocaa location in Malden ,but I love the fruit teas there and the Oreo Brown Sugar drink. They sell delicous fried chicken. Although, it takes a while for the fried chicken, it is very worth the wait because they make it fresh. The interior inside is adorable. It is a perfect hangout spot. My only complaint is the Prices are high and due to covid it went higher, but the quality makes it worth it at times.

New York's Pizza Malden

Category Score
Food 10
Interior 5
Prices/Affordability 9

New York Pizza is an Italian Pizza place. The subs and Zita alfredo is to die for. They add alot of alfredo sauce to the dish. Also, the prices are pretty good. I love going here when I want to eat good quality food, but not break the bank. I dont eat inside because there is AC and the kitchen is next the seating, so it gets very hot. The tables are very close together and the lighting is awful. Most of the time I do to go order.

Tex-Mex BBQ & Tequeria

Category Score
Food 10
Interior N/A
Prices/Affordability 7

I would honestly recommend every item in the menu here. I love the tacos and tortas espically. I always order the Tacos de Pastor. Since, it is a food truck, they make the food right when you order, so its always warm and fresh. The workers are all latinos and make it very authentic food. My only complaint is some plates portions are smaller than I would like and the prices are pretty high. However, a lot of tacos places food places have gone higher due to the price of meat going up.
